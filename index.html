My Garage

My Current Car: Golf MK7 2.0 TDI

This Golf is my first car. It does its job, it starts every time, it's solid, whatever. That's about it.

It's one of those typical European diesel daily cars. LED headlights, decent tech, nothing really exciting. I don't hate it, but I also don't feel anything for it.

It just doesn't feel like *me*. It's more like something I ended up with, not something I actually wanted.

I mean originally i wanted an s60 T5 but the whole fam HAD to get involved and say that it was too expensive to run

Im kind of attached I guess? I mean its my first car, but i could trade for a gasoline car I would, something of equal value of course

What I actually want is something that feels alive when you drive it. Not just "practical".

I mean I could go on a whole rant about why I dont really want it but in the end it comes down to the fact that its a diesel and I usually drive shorter distances (Plus I just like every characteristic of gas engine cars more)

Am looking to stage 1 tune it since apparently that boosts fuel economy and it should make the driving feel more engaging

Dream Car: Nissan 350Z

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.5L V6 VQ35
  • Power: 287 hp
  • Transmission: Manual 6-speed
  • 0-60 mph: 5.9 seconds
  • Emotion? All of it!

The 350Z is the opposite of my Golf in every way I actually care about.

That VQ35 sound is insane. It doesn't just "run", it actually feels like it has character ykwim???. Every video, every time I hear it, it just hits differently. Even if it does sound like a trumpet, its MY future trumpet and i adore it.

The design is perfect to me. Nothing overcomplicated. Just wide, aggressive, and clean in a way modern cars don't really do anymore. yk its just mega clean. Especially the new Z with that MEGA design

This whole Z platform just feels right to me. Raw, simple, no fake luxury nonsense. just pure sportscar

My dad doesn't really get it. He keeps saying it's a two-seater and that I just want it to show off or whatever. But that's not even the point.

I don't care. It's still the car I want. End of story.
